ABOUT THE ARCHIVE

Skated and reviewed by Subin

I am an adult figure skater documenting every rink I personally visit. These reviews focus on the details that matter during an actual practice session: the ice, the space, the atmosphere, and how easy it is to keep coming back.

All opinions are personal and independent. Prices and schedules may change after the date of review.
