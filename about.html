ABOUT SUBIN

A skater, a researcher,
and a collector of rinks.

I started figure skating on September 10, 2024. Since then, each rink has become part practice space, part travel memory, and part personal archive.

MY STORY

Why this archive exists

I am an adult figure skater from Korea and a member of the Northwestern University figure skating club. Outside the rink, I am pursuing a PhD in civil engineering.

My dream is to visit as many ice rinks as possible. I currently skate mostly in the United States and Korea, but I hope this archive eventually grows across countries and continents.

I review only rinks I have personally visited. I pay attention to the details that matter during a real practice session: ice quality, usable practice space, facilities, value, atmosphere, and how easy it is to return.
